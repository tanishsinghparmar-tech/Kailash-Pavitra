Heritage & Legacy

Our Journey
Since 1972

Five decades of purity, craftsmanship, and trust — woven into every grain we grind.

The Beginning

Founded in 1972 in Kota, Rajasthan — a small chakki mill with a big dream of bringing pure, freshly-ground flour to every Indian kitchen.

1972
1985

Growing Roots

Word spread through generations of families in Kota who trusted our traditional stone-ground process. Demand grew, but the quality never changed.

Expanding the Vision

Introduced pure Indian spices — Haldi, Mirchi, Dhaniya, and Garam Masala.

2000
2010

Health-First Innovation

Launched Multigrain Atta, High Protein Atta, and Sugar Free Atta.

Legacy Continues

Thousands of families across Rajasthan now rely on Kailash Pavitra Atta.
